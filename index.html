Coding Hackathon

Event Details

The 24-hour Coding Hackathon challenges students to develop innovative software solutions, test problem-solving skills, and collaborate in teams.

How to Register

  • Fill the registration form below with all required details.
  • Click “Send OTP” and verify your registration.
  • Once verified, you will appear in the participants’ list.

Venue Details

University Tech Hub, Main Auditorium, Block A, City Campus. Registration desk opens at 9:00 AM on event day.

How Event Validates

  • Participants attempt coding challenges within 24 hours.
  • Scores are calculated based on correct and attempted questions.
  • Top 3 scorers are selected as winners and notified via email & mobile.

Community Health Camp

🏥 Community Health Camp

Free Health Screening for a Healthier Community

📌 Event Overview

This Community Health Camp provides free medical screening to help detect health issues early and promote preventive care.

📍 Event Place & Schedule

Venue: Community Center, Lakeside Town

Date: March 15, 2026

Time: 9:00 AM – 5:00 PM

🧪 Tests Involved

  • Blood Pressure
  • Blood Sugar
  • Cholesterol
  • Vision Check
  • BMI

⚙️ Health Camp Process

  1. Participant registration
  2. Multiple health tests conducted
  3. Results recorded (Positive / Negative)
  4. Doctor consultation & advice
  5. Health summary provided
